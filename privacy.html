Privacy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

What Pathwise uses

Pathwise uses the résumé or experience and target role you provide to generate career guidance. That content is sent to the AI service for the requested analysis. Pathwise requests are configured not to be stored by the AI service.

What is saved

Your résumé text is not saved by Pathwise merely because you analyze it. If you choose Save this plan, Pathwise saves the resulting career plan, roadmap progress, and account-linked plan details in Supabase so you can return to them.

Your account

Supabase manages sign-in and account authentication. Pathwise uses your email address and profile name only to provide your account and saved-plan experience.

Your choices

You can delete saved plans from within Pathwise. Do not include information you would not want analyzed, and review every AI suggestion before using it in an application or interview.

Contact

For questions about this project, contact the project creator through the GitHub repository.
